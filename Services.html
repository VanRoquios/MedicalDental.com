MEDICAL/DENTAL UNIT

    The Heart of WVSU-PC

    SERVICES

    The Medical-Dental Unit provides the basic functional healthcare services to all WVSU-PC populace.
    Related task done includes the facilitation of the physical examination of students as part of the enrolment procedure,
    sport fest and OJT requirement and the annual physical examination of school personnel,
    making referrals of severe cases that needs medical treatment and management such as with PTB ( DOTS ),
    promotion of health and wellness of WVSU-PC community particularly monitoring the food served
    by the food providers in the School Canteen.
